BAO CAO NGAN GON - FLICKR8K IMAGE-TEXT
Project: ImageBind-style contrastive learning

1. Tong quan
- Dataset: Flickr8k
- Modality: Image + Text
- Cap du lieu: image-caption positive pairs
- Mo hinh: simplified ImageBind-style image-text contrastive learning
- Muc tieu: dua embedding cua anh va caption ve cung mot khong gian chung, danh gia bang bai toan retrieval hai chieu.

2. File output da doc
- flickr8k_train_history.json: log train theo epoch.
- flickr8k_learning_curve.png: bieu do loss theo epoch.
- flickr8k_eval_results.json: ket qua evaluate retrieval.
- flickr8k_summary.json: bang tong hop metric chinh.
- flickr8k_ablation_temperature.json: benchmark nhanh temperature.
- flickr8k_retrieval_examples.png: vi du truy van image-to-text dinh tinh.

3. Thiet lap train
- So epoch: 1
- Temperature chinh: 0.07
- So batch train: 1012
- Thoi gian train: 2501.66 giay (~41.7 phut)
- Train loss sau epoch 1: 3.0196

4. Ket qua retrieval tren test set
- So anh test: 810
- So cap caption test: 4049

Image -> Text:
- Recall@1  = 1.98%
- Recall@5  = 5.80%
- Recall@10 = 8.52%

Text -> Image:
- Recall@1  = 1.61%
- Recall@5  = 5.75%
- Recall@10 = 9.93%

5. Ablation temperature nhanh
Cac gia tri da thu: 0.05, 0.07, 0.10; moi gia tri train 100 batches.
- Temperature co train loss thap nhat: 0.1 (loss = 3.3670)
- Best Image->Text R@1 trong ablation: tau = 0.07 (0.37%)
- Best Text->Image R@1 trong ablation: tau = 0.1 (0.69%)
- Nhan xet: ablation chi chay 100 batches nen chua du manh de ket luan chac chan. Tuy nhien tau 0.07/0.10 cho ket qua on hon tau 0.05 trong phan lon metric.

6. Nhan xet tu bieu do va retrieval examples
- Learning curve chi co 1 diem vi moi train 1 epoch, nen chua danh gia duoc xu huong hoi tu lau dai.
- Retrieval examples cho thay co mot so truy van dung gan dung, nhung nhieu anh van tra ve caption khong lien quan. Dieu nay phu hop voi metric Recall@K con thap.
- Ket qua hien tai moi la baseline chay duoc tren Kaggle, chua phai ket qua toi uu.

7. Danh gia chung
Mo hinh da dap ung muc tieu toi thieu: co train tu dau tren dataset image-text, co evaluate tren test split va co ablation temperature. Tuy nhien performance con thap do chi train 1 epoch, dataset nho, backbone/encoder don gian va chua dung pretrained CLIP/ImageBind. Day la ket qua hop ly de dua vao bao cao nhu mot baseline implementation.

8. Huong cai thien
- Train them nhieu epoch, toi thieu 5-10 epoch neu Kaggle cho phep.
- Thu dung pretrained image/text encoder de tang chat luong embedding.
- Mo rong ablation: temperature, batch size, learning rate, projection head.
- Them hard-negative mining hoac caption augmentation.
- Bao cao nen trinh bay Recall@1/5/10 cho ca hai chieu Image->Text va Text->Image, kem learning curve va retrieval examples.
